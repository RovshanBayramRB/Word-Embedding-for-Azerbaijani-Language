Senior Design Project is an application of technical, communication, personal, and team skills. Oral and written communication of technical information, including progress reports, technical memos, final report, and oral presentations. Students work in small groups over two semesters to identify an opportunity, define the problem, analyze competing needs and requirements, perform prior work searches, develop alternative designs, carry out cost analyses, and select and implement a design solution. The course is a stepping stone for students to learn about engineering research, design and implementation.
